About Us

About ProgTeam@NYU

The ProgTeam is a place for students interested in solving problems to practice their skills, learn new ones and share their knowledge and passion with others.

Whether you are preparing for technical interviews, practicing for programming contests, or solve problems for fun, you should find like-minded students in ProgTeam.

Discord Server: We have a Discord Server for discussions. Click on the link to join us!

Kattis: We use Kattis as our current practice platform.

Practice Contests: We organize weekly in-person practices and contests during regular semesters. They are announced in our Discord Server. Unless otherwise indicated, those contests are hosted on Kattis (see above for how to gain access to the platform).

Faculty Adviser: Professor Yang Tang.

Location: Warren Weaver Hall (CIWW), 251 Mercer St, New York, NY 10012

Students from any campus are welcome to join the ProgTeam.
